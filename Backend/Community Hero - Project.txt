Communities face issues like:
Potholes
Water leakage
Garbage overflow
Broken street lights
Damaged roads
Open manholes
Illegal dumping
Fallen trees
Drainage blockage

People usually:
Don't know where to complain.
Complain multiple times.
Can't track progress.
Don't know if the issue was fixed.

The challenge is to build an AI-powered platform that helps citizens identify, report, validate, track, and resolve community issues through collaboration and intelligent automation.

FEATURES:-

Feature 1: Smart Image Understanding
User uploads: road.jpg, speak, write problem as a text
Gemini analyzes:
Category:
Pothole
Severity:
High
Estimated Diameter:
2.4 meters
Risk:
Very High
Suggested Department:
Road Maintenance
Summary:
Large pothole on busy road.
Immediate repair recommended.

Feature 2: Duplicate Detection
Suppose 50 people report the same pothole.
Instead of:
Issue #1
Issue #2
Issue #3
Issue #4
AI replies:
This issue already exists.
Do you want to support
existing complaint?
This avoids duplicate complaints.

Feature 3: Auto Priority
Instead of first-come-first-served:
AI calculates:
Severity × Traffic × People affected × School nearby × Hospital nearby -> Priority Score
Example
Garbage in park -> Priority = Medium
Broken bridge -> Priority = Critical

Feature 4: AI Summary
Citizen writes:
Road very bad.
Many accidents.
Please fix.
Very dangerous.

Gemini converts it into:
Road contains multiple deep potholes.
Several accidents have occurred.
Immediate maintenance required.

Feature 5: Department Routing
AI automatically sends issues to:
| Issue         | Department        |
| ------------- | ----------------- |
| Water leakage | Water Supply      |
| Garbage       | Sanitation        |
| Street light  | Electricity       |
| Road damage   | Road Department   |
| Tree fallen   | Forest Department |


Feature 6: AI Chat Assistant
Citizen asks:-
Why is my complaint delayed?
AI answers:-
Current status:
Waiting for municipal approval.
Estimated completion:
2 days.


Feature 7: Geo Mapping
Map shows
🟢 Resolved
🟡 In Progress
🔴 Critical
Clicking a marker displays:
Image
Status
Votes
Timeline


Feature 8: Community Verification
Instead of fake reports
Nearby users receive:
Can you verify this issue?
They answer:
✔ Yes
✖ No


Feature 9: Impact Dashboard
Shows
Total complaints
Resolved %
Average resolution time
Most common issue
Hotspot locations
Department performance


Feature 10: Predictive AI
Suppose
20 complaints
Same road
Same area
Last month
AI predicts:
High probability of new potholes
after heavy rainfall.


Feature 11: Voice Reporting
Citizen says
There is a broken streetlight near the bus stand.
AI converts speech to text, extracts:
Issue
Location
Summary


Feature 12: Emergency Detection
User uploads image.
Gemini detects:
Electric wire on road
Instead of normal priority:
⚠ Emergency
Immediate notification.
